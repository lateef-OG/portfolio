Nimcure - Nimcure is a digital care tool designed to promote adherence to TB treatment using technology, a combination of mobile and web apps. Patients and Caregivers manage the treatment process remotely through Video Observe Therapy(which is a variant of Direct Observe Therapy). Patients record videos of themselves taking their dosage and their caregivers can review and approve or decline remotely. vDot eliminates things like transportation costs, stress of travel and stigma.
